FAQs

How do I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets at our official website here.

Can I purchase walk-in tickets?

Yes, you can purchase tickets from the Information Counter from 7:00AM onwards. Sales of tickets for the current day will stop at 7:00PM.

Are there ATMs where I can withdraw money using the card issued from my country?

Yes, there are ATM machines available in the Park.

What is the maximum height limit for Adventure Theme Park Carpark?

The maximum height limit is 2.5 metres.
